Scallops

Description

Seared scallops, basted with garlic, butter, and lemon. A pairing to the Ribeye

Ingredients

  • Scallops
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Butter
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Dry the scallops with a paper towel, being sure to remove as much water from them as possible. Once dried, season them with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet. After the skillet is hot, add the olive oil.
  3. Place the scallops in the pan, in a circle clockwise.
  4. When you are done placing the last scallop, it should be time to flip the first one that you placed, and so on.
  5. After all the scallops are flipped, turn the heat to low and add the garlic, butter and lemon juice.
  6. Baste the scallops in the sauce for one minute and remove from heat.
  7. Place the scallops in a plate and pour the butter mixture from the pan over them.
