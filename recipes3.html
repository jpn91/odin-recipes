Butter Poached Lobster Tails

Description

Lobster tails poached in butter, heavy cream, garlic, and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • Lobster Tails
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Butter
  • Lemon Juice
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Start by lightly sauteing the garlic in a small amount of the butter
  2. After the garlic is starting to brown, add the rest of the butter.
  3. Continually whisk the butter until it is liquid
  4. Add some of the heavy cream, being sure to continue stirring>
  5. When the mixture is at a simmer, drop in the lobster tails (removed from the shell)
  6. Let the lobsters cook for no longer than 10 minutes
  7. Plate the lobster tails and add some of the mixture from the pot
  8. Add the lemon juice right before serving
